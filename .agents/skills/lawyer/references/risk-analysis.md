# Skill: Legal Risk Scoring

## Purpose
Produce a composite legal risk score for a company based on
all findings from contract review and compliance checks.

## Risk Scoring Matrix

Score each dimension 1-5 (1=low risk, 5=critical risk):

| Dimension | Score (1-5) | Weight | Weighted Score |
|-----------|-------------|--------|----------------|
| Data Privacy Compliance | _ | 25% | _ |
| ToS Fairness & User Rights | _ | 15% | _ |
| Litigation History | _ | 20% | _ |
| Regulatory Exposure | _ | 20% | _ |
| IP & Competitive Risk | _ | 10% | _ |
| Employment Law Signals | _ | 10% | _ |
| **Total** | | 100% | _ /5.0 |

## Risk Score Interpretation

| Score | Label | Recommendation |
|-------|-------|----------------|
| 1.0 – 2.0 | Low Risk | Standard due diligence sufficient |
| 2.1 – 3.0 | Moderate Risk | Flag for legal review before major action |
| 3.1 – 4.0 | High Risk | Require legal counsel before partnership/acquisition |
| 4.1 – 5.0 | Critical Risk | Do not proceed without full legal audit |

## Output
Always include the scored matrix table and an overall risk label
at the top of the legal report's Executive Summary.
