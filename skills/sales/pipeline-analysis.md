# Skill: Sales Pipeline & GTM Analysis

## Purpose
Decode a company's sales motion and pipeline structure
from their public digital presence.

## GTM Signal Detection

### Self-Serve / PLG Signals (scan pricing and homepage)
- Free tier or freemium offering visible on pricing page?
- "Start for free" or "Try for free" CTA on homepage?
- No sales contact required for signup?
- Usage-based upgrade prompts in feature descriptions?
- Viral mechanics: "invite your team", sharing features?

### Inside Sales Signals
- "Book a demo" as primary or secondary CTA?
- "Talk to sales" link?
- SDR/AE/BDR job postings?
- Mid-market focus in case studies?
- 30-day free trial (vs. freemium — more sales-assisted)?

### Enterprise Motion Signals
- "Contact us for pricing" (no self-serve)?
- Security/compliance page (enterprise requirement)?
- SSO, SCIM, audit logs in feature list?
- Named Fortune 500 customers?
- Legal/procurement language in ToS?
- Enterprise job titles in testimonials (CISO, VP Eng)?
- Multi-year contract language?

### Channel / Partner Signals
- Partner program page exists?
- Reseller or SI logos?
- AWS/Salesforce/HubSpot marketplace listing?
- "Built on / powered by" partner language?

## Open Role Velocity Analysis
Count job postings in these functions:
- Sales (AE, SDR, BDR, SE): growth = pipeline investment
- Customer Success (CSM, onboarding): growth = retention/expansion focus
- Marketing (demand gen, content): growth = awareness investment
- Engineering: growth = product expansion
- Finance/Ops: growth = scale/infrastructure investment

Map role ratio to GTM maturity stage.
